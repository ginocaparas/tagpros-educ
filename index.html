Joselle Salvador, M.Ed.

Joselle Salvador is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer.

Joselle is an educator who works at the intersection of creativity, learning, and media, intersecting visual/ new media literacy skills and merging creativity with new media technology in the classroom. She is a certified Apple Teacher and a Seesaw Ambassador.

She has a degree in Education and has a Master of Arts in educational psychology from the University of the Philippines.

Luis Pelea

Luis Pelea is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer.

His passion is integrating technology into education, so as to equip learners with 21st century skills. He is an Apple certified teacher, a Google certified educator, Seesaw Ambassador, and EdPuzzle certified coach.

He is currently completing his master’s degree at the Ateneo de Manila University.

Dominic Sales

Dominic Sales is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer.

He is passionate about robotics and is skilled at educational tools, mobile app design, website programming and 3D design, a speaker at the FIT conference, Edutech Asia and World education week. He is also an Apple Certified Teacher and a Google Certified Educator Level 1 and 2.

Nico Fos

Nico Fos is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer

He has a passion for advocating literacy and technology and is a leading curriculum writer and digital learning consultant in Filipino language and literature. He is an Apple Learning Leader, an edtech coach and regularly conducts training and workshops on seamless integration of technology in the curriculum.

He has a degree in English from Jose Rizal University and is a teacher fellow at Teach for the Philippines.

John Paul Viernes

John Paul Viernes is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer

He is an Edtech Leader and an ICT advocate who is passionate about integrating real-life projects to help students learn better with the aid of technology tools. He is also a Certified NEAP Learning Facilitator and a Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert-Master Trainer and was awarded as Edtech Coach of the Year by EduTech Asia He is currently the Education Program Supervisor for Department of Education, Laoag City.

He has a Bachelor's degree in Biology and a Master of Arts in Education Major in Biology from Mariano Marcos State University.

Joyce Zerda

Joyce Zerda is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer.

She is an educator, music coach, and tech integrator and her love for the creative arts and technology tools enables her to guide and empower her students in creating digital projects in her classes.

She is currently an assistant administrator, tech integrator, and arts program director at M. Zed Christian School. She's also an Apple Professional Learning Provider, an Apple Teacher, and an Apple Distinguished Educator.

She has a bachelor's degree in Music in Music Teacher Education from Siliman University and a Masters of Education in Innovation in Education from University of South Wales.

Lea Abarentos

Lea Abarentos is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer

She is an experienced web design and development instructor, an e-learning specialist and a freelance web/LMS developer and serves as a Project Manager for the Digital Technology Office of DLS-College of Saint Benilde. She’s an Apple Distinguished Educator and a Google Certified Educator Level 1 and 2. She’s also the project lead of inclusive projects such as Coding Club of the Benilde Deaf School and iLeadph.org (Innovative Learning Experiences Across Disabilities).

She has an Education degree majoring in Computer Technology and holds a master's degree in International Business.

Ivy Enaje

Ivy Enaje is a Tagpros Children teacher pioneer

She puts a premium in Filipino literacy through the lens of Culture based Literacy and 21st Century Learning. Her field of expertise ranges from curriculum design and development, module design, teacher training, and classroom instruction. She is a volunteer teacher, resource speaker, and curriculum consultant in different local yet reliable institutions.

She took her MA in Philippine Studies at the University of the Philippines, Diliman. Her thesis grounded her advocacy in women's health and gender studies.
