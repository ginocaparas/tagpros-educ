Our Team

Chris Manansala, M.Ed.

Christian Manansala is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer.

His interest in learning management and instructional design inspired him to design academic programs in STEM and Wildlife Education for educational institutions in the U.S., China, and the Philippines. He taught secondary mathematics for Virginia and California public schools for almost two decades; and was one of the pillars of the Filipino Language Program which is now an approved elective course offered at Stanford, UC Berkeley, and selected public schools in California. Because of his effort, he received honors from the State; and from United States Representative George Miller.

Chris earned his Bachelor’s degree from the University of the Philippines and has a Masters degree in Educational Technology from Touro University of California. He, additionally, received a degree in digital media from Duke University and has a certification of renewable and sustainable energy from North Carolina State University.

Francis Tuscano, M.Ed.

Jim Tuscano is a co-founder and Chief Programs Officer of TagPros.

He is a leading EdTech advocate and is a driving force in developing webinars for the professional development and accreditation of teachers in the Philippines for online distance learning.

Jim was cited as the best K12 teacher at the EdTech Advocate Awards, a finalist the Global Teacher Prize, founded the Kids Can Innovation camp and a member of the Asia Pacific Board of Apple Distinguished Educators (ADE). He is also a consultant to various educational institutions and organizations in the Philippines and hosts empowerED Podcast.

He earned his Masters degree from the University of the Philippines and has a Master of Education from the Ateneo de Manila University.

Alberto Garde Jr., MBA

Jo is a Co-Founder and Chief Marketing Officer.

He brings with him over 30 years of brand building and general management experience with leading global FMCG companies handling household brands like Oreo cookies, Tang beverages, Kraft cheeses and Sunkist.

Jo received his degree in Economics from Ateneo de Manila University and has a Master of Business Administration from the National University of Singapore School of Business.

Arturo Lasala

Arturo Lasala is the Treasurer and Chief Financial Officer

Art has more than 35 years of experience across multiple industries from banking, transportation, merchandising and recruitment.

He has a degree in Accountancy from Philippine School of Business Administration and is working on his MBA in Entrepreneurship from the University of Santo Tomas.

Cristina Palabrica, MLS

Tina Palabrica is Human Resource Officer.

Having worked in the administrative capacity for two decades, her work centered on improving working conditions and overall management support. Organizational skills were honed from her early years of working in an academic library and organizing many other private collections.

She finished both her undergraduate and graduate studies in Library & Information Studies from the University of the Philippines where she is presently connected.

Alberto Garde Jr., MBA

Kristy Rañada is the Senior Technology Officer.

Kristy currently is a research and development supervisor at AWS Philippines. She has worked as an actuarial analyst, associate and assistant. Additionally, she has worked as a Risk Management Assistant. Kristy is a certified Entry-Level Python Programmer and a Professional Scrum Master I and Professional Scrum Master II.

Kristy earned her bachelor’s degree in mathematics from the University of Santo Tomas and has an MBA from the University of the Philippines.
